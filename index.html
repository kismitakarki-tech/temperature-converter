Formulas & Common Conversions (Reference)

Quick formulas for area, data transfer rate, digital storage, energy, frequency, fuel economy, length, mass, plane angle, pressure, speed, temperature, volume and time.

Quantity Formula (copyable) Explanation / Notes
Area A = l × w
A = π r²		 Rectangle area (length × width). Circle area with radius r.
Data transfer rate bps = B/s × 8
B/s = bps ÷ 8		 Convert bytes-per-second to bits-per-second by ×8 (and reverse ÷8).
Digital storage KB = B ÷ 1000 · KiB = B ÷ 1024
MB = B ÷ 1e6 · GB = B ÷ 1e9		 Decimal (kB = 1000) vs binary (KiB = 1024) prefixes. Use consistent system.
Energy KE = ½ m v²
E = m c²		 Kinetic energy (mass m, speed v). Mass–energy equivalence (c = speed of light).
Frequency f = 1 / T
T = 1 / f		 f: frequency in Hz; T: period in seconds.
Fuel economy L_per_100km = 235.215 ÷ MPG_us
MPG_us = 235.215 ÷ L_per_100km		 Convert US miles-per-gallon ↔ liters per 100 km. Constant = 235.215.
Length cm = in × 2.54
m = ft × 0.3048		 inch to centimeter and foot to meter conversions.
Mass lb = kg × 2.2046226218
kg = lb ÷ 2.2046226218		 Kilogram ↔ pound. Constant rounded to typical precision.
Plane angle rad = deg × π / 180
deg = rad × 180 / π		 Degrees ↔ radians. π ≈ 3.141592653589793.
Pressure Pa = N ÷ m²
bar = Pa ÷ 100000		 Pascal is N/m². 1 bar = 100 000 Pa.
Speed km/h = m/s × 3.6
m/s = km/h ÷ 3.6		 Multiply by 3.6 to convert m/s to km/h (and divide to reverse).
Temperature °C = (°F − 32) × 5/9
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32		 Common Celsius ↔ Fahrenheit formulas. For Kelvin: K = °C + 273.15.
Volume m³ = L ÷ 1000
L = m³ × 1000		 1 cubic meter = 1000 liters.
Time h = s ÷ 3600
s = h × 3600		 Seconds ↔ hours. Use minutes (m = s ÷ 60) as needed.
